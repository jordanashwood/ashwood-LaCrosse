Why La Crosse?

La Crosse and the scenic views

Come to your senses and explore the La Crosse Region! See tri-state views while hiking ancient Grandad Bluff and admire compelling arts and history in local museums. Hear live music at festivals or by the local symphony and listen to unique tours within the Driftless Region. Taste exquisite cuisine at one-of-a-kind establishments and savor beverages along the Craft Beverage Trail. Smell fresh apple blossoms in the springtime along the Great River Road and take-in vistas of anglers and boats in the Mississippi River Valley.

Breweries

Best Breweries in La Crosse

Pearl Street Brewery

They've been at it since 1999. Crankin’ out award-winning beers and being part of the history of La Crosse. Brewing great beer isn’t everything — it’s the only thing. Go on in and check it out!

Address:

1401 Saint Andrew St.,
La Crosse, WI

What I like about it:

They've got a variety of great beer selections, and all are of high quality. The service is outstanding and they often have live music. It's a very friendly atmosphere.

Turtle Stack Brewery

A small independent craft brewery and tasting room located in historic downtown La Crosse. Produces high quality beer for locals and surrounding communities. Watch for their handcrafted beers on tap at local bars and restaurants!

Address:

125 2nd Street South,
La Crosse, WI

What I like about it:

They brew by a philosophy including quality, local sustainability, variety & innovation, and social responsibility.

608 Brewing Company

Their focus is on creating a fun, friendly, clean environment for all of our patrons. Harnessed their passion into a successful craft brewery for craft beer enthusiasts, newbies and anyone in between.

Address:

83 Copeland Ave,
La Crosse, WI

What I like about it:

Always a new beer to try! They're in cahoots with Taco Bros so that patrons have great food. They have outside seating, cozy atmostphere, and a friendly staff.
